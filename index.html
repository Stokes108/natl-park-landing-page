The Glories of Chataqua Park

Come join us for a hike in the forest of Colorado. Experience the magic of Boulder and the famous Flatirons. Countless local Colorado residents come expeirnce the magic of Chataqua park everyday including the local college students from the prestigous University of Colorado Boulder. Many people use this park to reconnect with nature and calm their anxious nerves. Come join us for a magical day of scenery, hiking and wildlife. Be prepared to see many local wildlife like deers, gunia pigs and a variety of birds. Hope to see you there!
